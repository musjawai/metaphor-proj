Short squeeze stocks have gained popularity among new traders in recent years, with potential plays gaining steam in 2021 and continuing into 2022. These stocks are characterized by high short interest, which occurs when a large number of investors have bet against a particular stock by borrowing and selling shares in the hopes of profiting from a decline in its price.

Some of the stocks to watch for potential short squeezes include Asset Entities, Lightning eMotors, Bullfrog AI, and Carvana. These companies have attracted attention due to their high short interest and the potential for a significant price increase if a short squeeze occurs.

A short squeeze happens when the price of a heavily shorted stock starts to rise, forcing short sellers to cover their positions by buying back the shares they borrowed. This buying pressure can lead to a rapid increase in the stock's price, causing short sellers to incur losses and further fueling the upward momentum.

For traders looking to capitalize on short squeeze opportunities, monitoring tangible metrics such as short interest ratio and short interest as a percentage of float can be helpful. These metrics provide insights into the level of short interest in a stock and the potential for a short squeeze to occur.

Overall, short squeeze stocks continue to be a focus for new traders seeking significant price movements. By paying attention to tangible metrics and identifying stocks with high short interest, traders can potentially profit from the volatility and momentum generated by short squeezes.
